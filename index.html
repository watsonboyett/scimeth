The Scientific Method

1

Observe

Define the question of interest.

2

Research

Gather existing information and data about the given observations.

3

Hypothesize

State what is believed will happen in a measurable way.

4

Experiment

Conduct unbiased, reproducible tests to evaluate the hypothesis.

5

Analyze

Review and interpret the data to draw conclusions.

6

Report

Communicate all previous steps in a complete and concise manner.
